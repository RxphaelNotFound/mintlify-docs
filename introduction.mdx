StudyFlow is an AI-powered homework and study platform built for secondary school and university students who want to genuinely understand the material — not just copy down an answer and move on. Whether you're untangling a tricky calculus problem at midnight, revising for a biology exam, or trying to make sense of a dense history passage, StudyFlow walks you through the reasoning step by step so the concept actually sticks. Every interaction is designed around one core belief: understanding is more valuable than the answer itself.

## What makes StudyFlow different

A lot of tools will hand you an answer. StudyFlow is built to do something harder: help you understand *why* that answer is correct and how to reach it yourself next time. Three principles shape everything about how the platform works.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Step-by-step guidance" icon="stairs">
    The AI tutor never just drops a final answer on you. Every response walks through the reasoning in clear, numbered steps — so you can follow along, ask follow-up questions, and build a mental model you can actually use on a test.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Full multi-subject coverage" icon="layer-group">
    From solving quadratic equations to analysing a Shakespeare sonnet to labelling a cell diagram, StudyFlow covers the full breadth of your coursework in one place. No switching between a dozen different apps.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Learns your pace" icon="chart-line">
    StudyFlow tracks your progress across sessions, adapts quiz difficulty to where you are right now, and builds personalised study plans that reflect your real strengths and gaps — not a one-size-fits-all schedule.
  </Card>
