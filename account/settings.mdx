StudyFlow's settings give you full control over how the app looks, behaves, and communicates with you. Whether you want to switch to dark mode, set up a daily study reminder, change the interface language, or review what data is stored against your account, every option lives in one place. Open Settings by selecting your avatar in the top-right corner on desktop and choosing **Settings**, or by tapping the gear icon in the mobile navigation menu.

---

<Accordion title="Profile">

Your profile controls how your name and avatar appear inside the app and in any sessions you share with others.

**Display name**
Your display name appears on your dashboard, shared session links, and any leaderboard features. To update it:
1. Go to **Settings → Profile**.
2. Select the **Display name** field and type your preferred name (3–40 characters).
3. Select **Save changes**.

**Avatar**
Upload a profile picture by selecting the camera icon on your current avatar. Supported formats are JPEG and PNG; maximum file size is 5 MB. StudyFlow automatically crops the image to a square. To remove your avatar and return to the default generated icon, select **Remove photo**.

**Email address**
Your email address is used for login, account notifications, and password resets. To change it:
1. Select **Change email** under your current address.
2. Enter your new email address and your current password to confirm.
3. StudyFlow sends a verification link to the new address. The change takes effect once you click that link.

Your old email address remains active for login until the new one is verified.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Appearance">

**Dark mode**
Toggle **Dark mode** to switch the entire interface to a dark colour scheme that reduces eye strain in low-light environments. The toggle applies immediately without a page reload. On mobile, you can also set dark mode to follow your device's system setting by selecting **Match system**.

**Font size**
Use the font size slider to scale all body text in the app. Options range from **Small** (90% of base size) to **Extra Large** (130% of base size). The default is **Medium**. This setting works independently of your device or browser's own text-size settings.

<Note>
  If you've also enabled **Larger text** under Accessibility settings, the two settings stack — Accessibility larger text increases the base size, and the Appearance font size slider scales relative to that new base.
</Note>

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Notifications">

StudyFlow can send you email digests and in-app reminders to keep your study habits consistent.

**Email digests**
Weekly summary emails include your session count, quiz accuracy, and streak status for the past 7 days, plus suggested topics to revisit based on your progress. To manage email digests:
1. Go to **Settings → Notifications**.
2. Toggle **Weekly progress digest** on or off.
3. Use the **Send on** dropdown to choose your preferred day of the week (default: Sunday).

**Study reminders**
Study reminders are push notifications (mobile) or browser notifications (desktop) that prompt you to open a session at a time you choose.

<Tip>
  Set a study reminder for the same time every day — even 15 minutes before bed. Consistent daily prompts are one of the most effective ways to build a lasting study habit, and they're the quickest way to protect your streak.
</Tip>

To enable study reminders:
1. Go to **Settings → Notifications → Study reminders**.
2. Toggle **Daily reminder** to **On**.
3. Select your preferred reminder time using the time picker.
4. If you're on mobile and haven't granted notification permissions yet, StudyFlow will prompt you to do so via your device's system dialog.

To disable reminders, toggle **Daily reminder** back to **Off**. You can also temporarily pause reminders for a set period (1 day, 1 week, or 1 month) using the **Snooze** option without fully turning them off.

**In-app notifications**
In-app notifications appear as a badge on the bell icon and include streak warnings, new feature announcements, and session expiry alerts. Toggle individual notification types on or off under **Settings → Notifications → In-app**.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Language & Region">

**Interface language**
The interface language controls all menus, labels, buttons, and system messages in the app. To change it:
1. Go to **Settings → Language & Region**.
2. Open the **Interface language** dropdown and select your preferred language.
3. Select **Save**. The app reloads in the new language immediately.

StudyFlow currently supports English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Mandarin Chinese, Japanese, and Arabic. Additional languages are added regularly — check the dropdown for the latest list.

**Default subject language**
The default subject language tells the AI tutor which language to use when generating explanations, quiz questions, and flashcard content. This can be set independently of the interface language — for example, you might prefer the app menus in English but want all Biology content generated in Spanish for language immersion practice.

To change the default subject language, select it from the **Content language** dropdown under Language & Region. You can also override this on a session-by-session basis by specifying a language when you start a new session.

**Timezone**
StudyFlow uses your timezone to calculate study streaks and schedule reminders accurately. It auto-detects your timezone on first login. To update it manually, use the **Timezone** dropdown and select your region and city.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Accessibility">

StudyFlow includes several accessibility options to ensure the app works well for every learner.

**High contrast mode**
High contrast mode increases the colour contrast ratio across all UI elements, making text and interactive components easier to distinguish. Toggle it on under **Settings → Accessibility → High contrast**.

**Larger text**
Enabling **Larger text** increases the base font size by 20% across the entire app. This is applied before the Appearance font-size slider, so the two settings work together if you need maximum text size.

**Reduce motion**
Toggle **Reduce motion** to disable non-essential animations — including page transitions, loading spinners, and chart animations — and replace them with instant cuts. This setting is recommended for users who experience discomfort from animated interfaces or who use screen readers that are disrupted by movement.

**Screen reader support**
StudyFlow's web app is built to WCAG 2.1 AA standards. All interactive elements include ARIA labels, and the tab order follows a logical reading sequence on every screen. If you encounter a screen reader compatibility issue, use the **Report an issue** link at the bottom of the Accessibility settings page to send details directly to the accessibility team.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Privacy & Data">

**Download your data**
You can export a full copy of all data StudyFlow holds about your account, including your session history, quiz results, progress data, profile information, and settings.

1. Go to **Settings → Privacy & Data → Download your data**.
2. Select **Request export**.
3. StudyFlow prepares your archive and emails you a download link within 24 hours. The link is valid for 7 days.

The export is a ZIP file containing your data in JSON and CSV formats.

**Clear progress data**
To reset all quiz scores, session history, and streaks without deleting your account, select **Clear all progress data** and confirm. See [Progress Tracking](/account/progress-tracking#resetting-or-clearing-progress) for more detail on what this affects.

**Delete your account**

<Warning>
  Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. All your saved sessions, progress data, quiz history, and profile information are immediately and permanently erased. StudyFlow cannot recover any data after an account is deleted. Download your data before proceeding if you want to keep a copy.
</Warning>

To delete your account:
1. Go to **Settings → Privacy & Data → Delete account**.
2. Read the confirmation notice detailing what will be removed.
3. Enter your password and type **DELETE** in the confirmation field.
4. Select **Permanently delete my account**.

Your account is deactivated immediately and all associated data is deleted within 30 days in accordance with StudyFlow's data retention policy. If your account is on an active paid subscription, the subscription is cancelled at the same time with no further charges.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Password & Security">

**Change your password**
1. Go to **Settings → Password & Security → Change password**.
2. Enter your current password, then enter and confirm your new password.
3. Your new password must be at least 10 characters long and include at least one number and one special character.
4. Select **Update password**. All other active sessions are signed out automatically when your password changes.

**Forgot your password?**
If you can't remember your current password, sign out and use the **Forgot password** link on the [login page](https://app.studyflow.app/login) to receive a reset link by email.

**Two-factor authentication (2FA)**
Two-factor authentication adds a second verification step at login, protecting your account even if your password is compromised.

To enable 2FA:
1. Go to **Settings → Password & Security → Two-factor authentication**.
2. Select **Set up 2FA**.
3. Choose your preferred method: **Authenticator app** (recommended, works with Google Authenticator, Authy, and any TOTP-compatible app) or **SMS** (a code sent to your verified mobile number).
4. Follow the on-screen instructions to link your authenticator app or verify your phone number.
5. Save the backup codes shown at the end of setup in a secure location. These codes let you access your account if you lose access to your 2FA method.

To disable 2FA, go to **Settings → Password & Security → Two-factor authentication** and select **Remove 2FA**. You'll be asked to verify with your current 2FA method before it's removed.

**Active sessions**
The **Active sessions** panel under Password & Security lists every device and browser currently signed in to your account, including the device type, approximate location, and time of last activity. Select **Sign out** next to any session to remotely revoke that device's access, or select **Sign out all other sessions** to log out everywhere except your current device.

</Accordion>
