StudyFlow is free to join — no credit card required. Whether you're a student tackling nightly homework or preparing for exams, creating your account takes just a few minutes and unlocks AI-powered help across Mathematics, English, Biology, Science, History, Geography, Art, and more. Follow the steps below to get set up and ready to learn.

## Sign Up for StudyFlow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the sign-up page">
    Open your browser and navigate to [https://app.studyflow.app/signup](https://app.studyflow.app/signup). You can sign up from any modern desktop or mobile browser — no app download is required to get started.

    You have two ways to create your account:

    - **Email and password** — enter your email address, choose a secure password, and continue.
    - **Continue with Google** — click the **Continue with Google** button to use your existing Google account. This skips the email verification step entirely.

    <Tip>
      Using Google sign-in is the fastest way to get started. Your StudyFlow display name and avatar are pre-filled from your Google profile, though you can change them at any time.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify your email address">
    If you signed up with an email and password, StudyFlow sends a verification message to the address you provided. Open the email and click **Verify my email** to confirm your account.

    <Note>
      The verification email usually arrives within one to two minutes. If you don't see it, check your spam or junk folder. You can request a new link from the sign-up confirmation screen by clicking **Resend verification email**.
    </Note>

    Once your email is verified, you are redirected back to StudyFlow to continue setting up your profile.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose your subjects and year level">
    During onboarding, StudyFlow asks you to select the subjects you study and your current year level. This information personalises your experience — the AI tailors explanations to your curriculum level, and your study plan suggestions reflect the subjects you actually need help with.

    Select all subjects that apply from the list:

    - Mathematics
    - English
    - Biology
    - Science
    - History
    - Geography
    - Art
    - Additional languages

    You can update your subject selections at any time from **Account Settings → Subjects**.

    <Tip>
      Even if you only need help with one subject right now, selecting a few others lets StudyFlow surface relevant quiz and flashcard suggestions as you explore the platform.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set your display name and avatar (optional)">
    Give your account a personal touch by entering a display name and uploading a profile avatar. Your display name appears in saved sessions, study plans, and any shared notes.

    Both fields are optional — you can skip this step and come back to it later under **Account Settings → Profile**.

    Accepted avatar formats are JPG, PNG, and GIF, up to 5 MB.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete onboarding and enter StudyFlow">
    Click **Get started** to finish onboarding. StudyFlow takes you directly to your dashboard, where your personalised study plan and AI chat are ready to use. You have successfully created your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Log In on Multiple Devices

StudyFlow works in any modern browser and on iOS and Android mobile apps. Your account, saved sessions, and progress sync automatically across every device you use.

To log in, visit [https://app.studyflow.app/login](https://app.studyflow.app/login) or open the mobile app and tap **Log in**. Enter your email and password, or tap **Continue with Google** if that is how you signed up.

<Note>
  StudyFlow keeps you logged in on trusted devices. If you are using a shared or public computer, tick **Log out when I close the browser** on the login screen to prevent others from accessing your account.
</Note>

## Reset Your Password

If you forget your password, follow these steps to regain access:

1. Go to [https://app.studyflow.app/login](https://app.studyflow.app/login) and click **Forgot password?**
2. Enter the email address associated with your account and click **Send reset link**.
3. Open the email from StudyFlow and click **Reset my password**.
4. Enter and confirm your new password, then click **Save password**.

You are logged in automatically once the reset is complete.

<Tip>
  If you signed up with Google, you do not have a StudyFlow password. Use **Continue with Google** to log in and manage your password through your Google account settings instead.
</Tip>

## Account Security Tips

Keeping your account secure protects your study history, saved sessions, and personal information. Follow these best practices:

- **Use a strong, unique password.** Choose a password you don't use on any other site. A mix of uppercase letters, numbers, and symbols works best.
- **Enable Google sign-in for added security.** Google accounts support two-factor authentication, which gives your StudyFlow login an extra layer of protection.
- **Log out on shared devices.** Always log out after using StudyFlow on a school computer, library terminal, or any device that isn't yours.
- **Keep your email address up to date.** Your email is how StudyFlow sends password reset links and important account notifications. Update it under **Account Settings → Profile** if it ever changes.
- **Review active sessions.** Under **Account Settings → Security**, you can see all devices currently logged in to your account and revoke access to any you don't recognise.

<Note>
  StudyFlow never asks for your password by email or in-app chat. If you receive a suspicious message claiming to be from StudyFlow, do not click any links — contact support at support@studyflow.app.
</Note>
