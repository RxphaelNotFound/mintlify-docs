StudyFlow is designed to make studying clearer, faster, and more effective — but it's natural to have questions before you dive in. Below you'll find answers to the things students ask most, organised into sections so you can jump straight to what you need.

## Getting Started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is StudyFlow free to use?">
    StudyFlow offers a free tier that gives you access to AI chat explanations, basic quizzes, and flashcard creation with a generous daily usage limit. A **StudyFlow Plus** subscription unlocks unlimited AI sessions, photo uploads, full study plans, note summarisation, and detailed progress tracking. You can start for free at [app.studyflow.app](https://app.studyflow.app) — no credit card required.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What devices and browsers does StudyFlow support?">
    StudyFlow works in any modern browser on desktop and laptop computers, including the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. On mobile, StudyFlow is available as a native app for iOS (iPhone and iPad, iOS 15 or later) and Android (Android 9 or later). The browser version is also fully responsive, so you can use it on your phone without installing anything if you prefer.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need to install anything?">
    No installation is required to use StudyFlow in your browser — just visit [app.studyflow.app](https://app.studyflow.app) and sign up. If you want the best experience on a phone or tablet, we recommend downloading the StudyFlow app from the App Store or Google Play, which gives you faster load times, offline flashcard review, and camera integration for photo uploads.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does StudyFlow have a dark mode?">
    Yes — StudyFlow includes a built-in dark mode that's easy on your eyes during late-night study sessions. To switch between light and dark mode, click your **profile avatar** in the top-right corner and select **Appearance**, then choose **Dark**, **Light**, or **System** (which automatically follows your device's display setting). Your preference is saved to your account and syncs across all your devices, so you only need to set it once.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## AI & Learning

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Will StudyFlow just give me the answer?">
    StudyFlow is built to help you *understand*, not just hand you answers. When you ask about a problem, the AI walks you through the concepts, reasoning steps, and methods behind the solution so you can apply that knowledge yourself. If you're stuck on a specific step, you can ask for a hint rather than a full explanation — and the AI will adjust how much guidance it gives based on how you respond.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How accurate is the AI? Can it make mistakes?">
    StudyFlow's AI is highly capable across all supported subjects and is regularly evaluated for accuracy. That said, AI systems can occasionally make errors — particularly with complex multi-step maths problems, ambiguous questions, or very specialised topics. Always cross-reference important answers with your textbook, class notes, or teacher, especially before an exam. If you spot an incorrect answer, you can flag it directly in the session using the thumbs-down button so our team can review it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What subjects and languages does StudyFlow support?">
    StudyFlow currently supports **Maths, English, Biology, Science, History, Geography, and Art** as core subjects. For language learning, StudyFlow supports practice and explanation in a wide range of languages including Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Mandarin, Japanese, Arabic, and more. If you're unsure whether your language or subject is covered, start a session and ask — the AI will let you know if a topic is outside its current scope.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I ask follow-up questions in the same session?">
    Absolutely. Each StudyFlow chat session maintains full context, so you can ask follow-up questions, request a different explanation style, ask for more examples, or go deeper into any part of the topic without starting over. You can say things like *"Can you explain that differently?"* or *"Now show me a harder example"* and the AI will respond with your conversation history in mind. Sessions can be saved and resumed later too.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I upload a photo of my homework?">
    On the web app, click the **camera icon** in the chat input bar and select a photo from your device. On mobile, tapping the same icon lets you choose between your photo library or taking a new photo with your camera. For the best results, make sure the image is well-lit, in focus, and that all text is clearly visible within the frame. The AI will read the question or problem in the photo and respond as if you had typed it out.

    <Tip>
      Crop your photo to show just the relevant question before uploading — this helps the AI focus on exactly what you need help with and produces faster, more accurate responses.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Account & Data

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I reset my password?">
    Go to [app.studyflow.app](https://app.studyflow.app) and click **Log in**, then select **Forgot password?** beneath the password field. Enter the email address associated with your account and we'll send you a reset link within a few minutes. The link expires after 30 minutes, so use it promptly. If you don't see the email, check your spam or junk folder — and make sure you're checking the correct inbox if you have multiple email addresses.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are my study sessions saved automatically?">
    Yes — StudyFlow automatically saves your sessions as you work, so you won't lose progress if your browser closes or your connection drops. Saved sessions appear in the **My Sessions** panel on the left-hand sidebar. You can rename, organise into folders, or delete sessions from there at any time. On the free tier, you can store up to 20 sessions; StudyFlow Plus members have unlimited session storage.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long are my sessions stored?">
    Sessions on the free tier are stored for **90 days** from the date of last activity. After that, inactive sessions are automatically deleted. StudyFlow Plus subscribers have their sessions stored indefinitely for as long as their subscription is active. You can export any session as a PDF at any time from the session menu — a good habit before important exams.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use StudyFlow on multiple devices?">
    Yes. Your StudyFlow account syncs across all your devices in real time. Sign in with the same email and password on your laptop, tablet, and phone, and all your sessions, flashcards, study plans, and progress data will be available everywhere. There is no limit on the number of devices you can be signed into simultaneously.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Privacy & Safety

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is my data private? Who can see my sessions?">
    Your sessions are private by default and are never shared with other users. StudyFlow staff may access session data only to investigate reported issues, improve AI accuracy, or comply with legal obligations — and access is logged and audited. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. For full details on how your data is collected, stored, and used, please read our [Privacy Policy](https://app.studyflow.app/privacy).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is StudyFlow safe for students under 13?">
    StudyFlow complies with applicable children's privacy regulations, including COPPA in the United States and the UK Children's Code. Students under 13 require verified parental or guardian consent to create an account. Parents and guardians can manage consent and review account activity through the **Family Dashboard**, accessible from account settings. If you believe a child has registered without consent, please contact us at [support@studyflow.app](mailto:support@studyflow.app) and we will remove the account promptly.

    <Note>
      Schools deploying StudyFlow for students under 13 should contact [support@studyflow.app](mailto:support@studyflow.app) to set up a managed school account with the appropriate safeguards in place.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
