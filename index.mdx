## How StudyFlow Works

StudyFlow is built around one idea: understanding beats memorising. Instead of handing you the answer, the AI guides you through the reasoning so you actually learn — and remember — the material.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a free account">
    Sign up with your email or Google account. No credit card required to get started.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ask a question or upload a photo">
    Type your question directly, or snap a photo of your homework and upload it. StudyFlow reads handwritten and printed text.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get a step-by-step explanation">
    The AI breaks down the problem into clear, logical steps. Ask follow-up questions any time — the conversation continues until you understand.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Practise and track your progress">
    Generate a quiz, create flashcards, or build a study plan. Your progress is saved so you can pick up exactly where you left off.
  </Step>
</Steps>
